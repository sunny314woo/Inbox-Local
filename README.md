# Inbox Local

> Local-first AI Knowledge Inbox for ChatGPT and Gemini

Inbox Local is a privacy-focused desktop application designed to organize, search, and manage AI conversations exported from ChatGPT and Gemini.

Instead of leaving valuable knowledge scattered across hundreds of AI chats, Inbox Local turns conversations into a structured, searchable, and reusable knowledge base that stays entirely on your own device.

---

## Features

### Structured Import

Import AI conversations in a structured format from:

- ChatGPT
- Gemini
- ChatGPT Outline & Export Extension

Preserve:

- Conversation hierarchy
- Headings
- Bookmarks
- Metadata

---

### Knowledge Organization

Organize imported content with:

- Tags
- Categories
- Bookmarks
- Structured outlines

Build your own personal AI knowledge library.

---

### Full-Text Search

Quickly locate information across all imported conversations.

Search by:

- Title
- Content
- Tags
- Keywords

---

### Local-First

Your data stays on your computer.

Inbox Local:

- Does not upload conversations
- Does not require cloud storage
- Does not track usage
- Does not require subscriptions

---

### Structured Writing

Imported conversations remain editable and reusable.

Create:

- Research notes
- Project documentation
- Knowledge collections
- Draft articles
